Cutting and Preparation

Our manufacturing process begins with precise fabric cutting and preparation. Using state-of-the-art cutting machinery and traditional techniques, we ensure each piece is cut to exact specifications. Our experienced team carefully inspects fabrics for quality and consistency before the cutting process begins.

Precision Cutting

Computer-aided cutting ensures accuracy and minimal waste

Quality Fabrics

02

Sewing and Assembly

Our skilled craftspeople bring decades of experience to the sewing process. Each shirt is carefully assembled using high-quality threads and precise stitching techniques. We maintain traditional craftsmanship while utilizing modern sewing technology for consistency and durability.

Expert Craftsmanship

Skilled artisans with up to 50 years of experience

Reinforced Stitching

Double-stitched seams for enhanced durability

03

Quality Control

Every shirt undergoes rigorous quality control inspection at multiple stages. Our quality assurance team checks for stitching consistency, fabric integrity, sizing accuracy, and overall construction quality. Only shirts that meet our exacting standards proceed to the next stage.

Multi-Stage Inspection

Comprehensive quality checks at every production phase

Sizing Accuracy

Precise measurements to ensure perfect fit

04

Final Touches

The finishing process includes pressing, button attachment, label application, and final detailing. Our attention to detail ensures each shirt has a professional appearance and premium feel. Special care is taken with collar shaping and cuff finishing for that perfect business look.

Professional Pressing

Steam pressing for crisp, professional appearance

Premium Details

Quality buttons and refined finishing touches

Packaging and Delivery

Completed shirts are carefully packaged using protective materials to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. We coordinate with reliable logistics partners to ensure timely delivery to our clients worldwide. Each package is tracked and monitored throughout the shipping process.

Protective Packaging

Careful packaging to maintain quality during transit

Global Shipping
