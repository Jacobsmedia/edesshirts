ABOUT

Our Story

Manifesto

At EDES LLC, our journey began around 3 decades ago as a family business in the heart of Albania. Our founders envisioned creating a manufacturing company dedicated to producing medium to high-quality men's shirts, setting the foundation for what would become a trademark of excellence in the industry. Since our inception in 1998, we have been faithful in our commitment to prevail the art of making shirts and project management, in order to deliver a product and a service that not only meets but exceeds expectations.

Our factory, strategically located to leverage both location and global tendency, is equipped with superior machinery and staffed with a highly-proficient team that has up to 50 years of working experience in the sector. These assets ensure that we meet the demands of our clients without ever compromising on quality. Each piece we produce is proof to our promise.

Specialising in private label manufacturing, we offer customised solutions tailored to the client's needs. This method has enabled us to build a rich portfolio of over 1,000 brands, that we call our most valuable patchwork project.

At EDES, we are more than a manufacturer; we are partners in your success. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, cherishing each collaboration, and driving mutual growth.

Explore our private label production services

As we look to the future, our guiding principles remain rooted in our past. We continue to sew together decades of work, passion, and practice, with our favourite threads being the understanding of our clients' needs and attention to detail. We remain open to new talents and partnerships, embracing the belief that "there is nothing in this world that cannot be improved."
